Frequently Asked Questions

Register for an account on the HMS Portal, then contact the hostel office. An administrator will assign you to an available room based on your gender and available capacity.

Pay via bank transfer or mobile money, then log in to the HMS Portal and submit your payment reference number under "Submit Payment". The hostel office will verify your payment within 24–48 hours.

Room assignments are currently done manually by hostel administrators based on availability. You may indicate your preference to the hostel office, and they will try to accommodate requests where possible.

If all beds are occupied, you will be placed on a waiting list. The hostel office will contact you as soon as a space becomes available. Check the Hostels page regularly for live availability updates.
