ABOUT US

Who We Are

The HMS Portal is Cavendish University Uganda's official student accommodation management system — built to simplify hostel allocation, payments, and administration.

OUR PURPOSE

Simplifying Student Accommodation

The Hostel Management System (HMS) was developed as part of a final-year project at Cavendish University Uganda to address the manual, paper-based processes that previously governed student accommodation.

Our platform allows students to view room availability, submit accommodation payments, and track their allocation status — all from a single online portal accessible anywhere on campus.

Our Mission

To provide a seamless, transparent, and efficient digital platform for managing student hostel accommodation.

Our Vision

A fully paperless hostel administration that empowers both students and management.

Transparency

Real-time room availability and payment status visible to every student.

Accountability

Every allocation and payment is recorded, tracked, and auditable.

What the System Offers

A complete digital workflow for everyone involved in student accommodation.

Hostel & Room Inventory

Manage hostels, blocks, and individual rooms with real-time occupancy tracking and capacity enforcement.

Smart Allocation

Administrators assign rooms to students with automatic capacity validation and gender-matching checks.

Payment Tracking

Students submit payment records with reference numbers. Managers verify and approve with a full audit trail.

Reports & Analytics

Generate allocation, payment, and occupancy reports. Export to CSV for external analysis.

Role-Based Access

Separate secure portals for students and hostel managers, each with the tools they need.

Mobile Friendly

Fully responsive design — access your hostel information from any device on or off campus.
